Subscribe is the best way to manage your Google Reader subscriptions from your iPhone or iPod touch. Subscribe lets you subscribe to and unsubscribe from your Google Reader feeds.

Get it on the App Store

Features

  • Subscribe to great new feeds
  • Unsubscribe from crap feeds
  • Simple interface
  • Retina display ready
  • View details of each individual feed
  • Google account info securely stored in keychain

Requirements

  • Subscribe works with the iPhone or iPod touch running iOS 5.0 or later
  • Google account
